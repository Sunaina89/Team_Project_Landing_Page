Story Time Haven Podcasts!

A childrens' podcast web app which allows parents to register and access a secure area featuring an educational and entertaining ABC story series for kids. This targets kids from ages 2 - 4. There is a registration page that will give parents access to the podcast. There is also an option to watch the video version of the story series on YouTube. The site will include links to the YouTube channel. A few audio episodes will be available for free while other episodes will be for paid members only. Children will build their vocabulary through these fun and educational stories. On the platform, there will be cards representing each podcast episode and matching activity sheets when you click on the card. Parents may download and print these worksheets for their children. Additionally, there will be a coming soon tab featuring stories for 5 to 6 year olds.
